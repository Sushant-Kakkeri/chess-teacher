The basic idea

  • Two players, White and Black, take turns moving one piece at a time.
  • You win by checkmating the opponent's king — putting it under attack with no safe escape.
  • You capture a piece by moving one of your own onto its square (except the special "en passant" pawn capture).
  • Click a piece above to see exactly where it's allowed to move, highlighted on the board.
  • Once this feels familiar, head to Play — the app will flag good and bad moves as you go.
